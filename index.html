Zodiac

The zodiac is the general name for the sky, which includes all the planets and stars. While creating the star chart, the Zodiac is used and the signs are studied. Different constellations represent the twelve signs of the zodiac. Every celestial body has a sphere of motion. The movement of planets and stars reveals transits in the natal chart. Sun, ascendant and moon signs and their degrees and houses are determined by the movements of the planets in the Zodiac.

Houses

Each house in astrology rules a different aspect of life. Houses are an important aspect of astrology that should be understood and studied just as much as signs and planets. The energy of the planets can manifest in various ways. The effects of this energy are shown by the houses. Planets in a game characters represent the different ways in which these actors can play their roles. There are 12 houses in the zodiac and their different characteristics.

Planets

There are eight planets taken into account by astrology. In order of magnitude of their mutual distance from the sun, these are; Mercury, Venus, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune and Pluto. A natal chart has to include the positions of certain lunar nodes of the zodiac. These nodes (north and south) are the points where a planet's orbit intersects the plane of the ecliptic.

Birth Chart

The birth chart, also known as the Star chart, is a two-dimensional diagram that shows the positions of all the planets in the Solar system, according to their birth time and location, at the moment when the person first breathes into the world. There are three main elements in the Natal Chart. These; signs, planets and houses.
